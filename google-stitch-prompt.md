# Google Stitch Prompt — aWARE by WARE AI Landing Page

---

Create a premium SaaS landing page for **aWARE**, a voice-activated AI desktop assistant by **WARE AI**.

## CRITICAL: DARK THEME ONLY

The ENTIRE page must use a dark theme. Every single section background must be dark — either #111111, #1A1A2E, or a purple-to-blue-to-green gradient. There must be ZERO white backgrounds, ZERO light gray backgrounds, ZERO light sections anywhere on the page. All text is white or light gray on dark backgrounds. This is non-negotiable.

---

## COLOR RULES (apply everywhere)

- **Page background:** #111111 (near-black) for every section
- **Text color:** #FFFFFF for headlines, #9CA3AF for body paragraphs
- **Accent gradient:** linear-gradient from #5B21B6 (purple) through #3B5BFF (blue) to #34D399 (green) — used on feature cards and CTA buttons
- **Highlight text:** #2563EB bright blue for emphasized words in headlines like "aWARE"
- **Voice command quotes:** #F6C547 golden-yellow italic text
- **All buttons:** solid #5B21B6 purple pill-shaped with white text, OR outlined with white border on dark background
- **All card backgrounds:** either the purple→blue→green gradient OR a slightly lighter dark (#1E1E2E) — never white, never light

---

## TYPOGRAPHY

Font: Inter or similar clean sans-serif. Headlines: 48–72px bold white. Body: 16–18px light gray (#9CA3AF). Everything on dark backgrounds.
