# Google Stitch Prompt — WARE AI Unified Landing Page

---

## Prompt:

Build a complete, single-page landing website for **WARE AI** — a company that makes both an **AI desktop voice assistant (aWARE)** and a **wearable AI device (WARE Series 100 & WARE Series PRO)**. The tagline is **"Effortless AI."**

---

### BRAND & COLOR PALETTE

- **Primary gradient:** Electric blue (#3B5BFF) → vivid green (#34D399), used on cards, buttons, hero accents, and feature panels. This gradient should appear prominently throughout — it's the signature brand look.
- **Dark mode base:** Deep charcoal/near-black background (#1A1A2E or #111111) for the majority of the page.
- **White sections:** Alternate between dark and clean white sections for contrast and visual rhythm.
- **Accent text color:** Bright blue (#2563EB) for highlighted keywords inside headlines.
- **Body text on dark:** Light gray (#A0AEC0) on dark backgrounds.
- **Body text on white:** Dark gray (#374151) on light backgrounds.
- **Typography:** Clean, modern sans-serif (Inter or similar). Headlines should be **very large and bold** (48–72px), body text 16–18px.

---

### LOGO & NAVIGATION

- **Logo** in the top-left: "WARE AI" in gradient text (blue-to-green) with a small heartbeat/waveform icon (∿) underneath and the sub-text "EFFORTLESS AI".
- **Sticky top navigation bar** with transparent background that gets a subtle frosted glass/blur effect on scroll.
- Nav items: **Home | App | Device | PRO Device | FAQs | Contact**
- **CTA button** in the nav: "HOW WILL YOU WARE IT?" — pill-shaped with the blue-to-green gradient fill, white text.

### DESIGN EFFECTS & INTERACTIONS

1. **Gradient cards with large rounded corners (20–30px border-radius)** — used extensively for feature sections, product showcases, and the contact form.
2. **Frosted glass navbar** — blur backdrop filter on scroll.
3. **Smooth scroll-triggered fade-in animations** — elements fade up as you scroll into view.
4. **Hover effects on buttons** — subtle scale-up (1.05x) and shadow deepening.
5. **Circular image frames** — lifestyle/use-case photos cropped into perfect circles.
6. **Paint splatter graphics** — behind product color showcase for artistic energy.
7. **Carousel/slider** — for product cards with left/right arrows.
8. **Gradient text** — the logo and select headlines use CSS gradient text (background-clip: text).
9. **Dark/light section alternation** — creates visual rhythm and breathing room.
10. **Large, confident typography** — headlines are oversized (48–72px) and bold, creating a premium tech feel.
11. **Soft colored background washes** — subtle gradient tints behind sections (light blue, light green, light purple) on white backgrounds.
12. **Rounded pill buttons** everywhere — consistent with the friendly, modern aesthetic.
13. **Icon grids** — feature icons and app integration icons displayed in clean grids.
14. **Responsive design** — mobile-first, stacking columns on small screens.

---

### OVERALL TONE

Premium, modern, tech-forward but approachable. Think Apple product page meets AI startup energy. The design should feel **clean, spacious, and confident** — lots of whitespace, bold headlines, and the signature blue-to-green gradient tying everything together. The page should seamlessly present both the **software product (aWARE desktop assistant)** and the **hardware product (WARE wearable devices)** as a unified ecosystem.
